HOMEGOINGHQ — ACCESSIBILITY STATEMENT

Entity: EdConsult LLC d/b/a HomegoingHQ (Michigan LLC) | Notice: 1098 Ann Arbor Rd W, #479, Plymouth, MI 48170 Last Updated: 7/18/2026 Version: 1.0

1. OUR COMMITMENT

HomegoingHQ is committed to making our Platform accessible to people with disabilities. We serve families during difficult times, and we want everyone to be able to use our tools. We are working toward conformance with the Web Content Accessibility Guidelines (WCAG) 2.1 Level AA.

2. MEASURES WE TAKE

We strive to: use semantic structure and labels; provide sufficient color contrast; support keyboard navigation; label form fields and interactive controls; provide text alternatives for meaningful images; and design compassionate, readable content. We consider accessibility in our design and development process.

3. ONGOING EFFORTS

Accessibility is an ongoing effort. We periodically review the Platform and work to remediate issues we identify or that are reported to us. Third-party content and embedded tools (for example, certain partner or provider components) may not be fully under our control.

4. FEEDBACK AND ASSISTANCE

If you encounter an accessibility barrier, or need assistance accessing any feature, please contact us and we will work to help and to address the issue:

Email: care@homegoinghq.com Mail: 1098 Ann Arbor Rd W, #479, Plymouth, MI 48170Please describe the issue, the page or feature, and your device/assistive technology so we can respond effectively.

5. ALTERNATIVE ACCESS

If you cannot complete a task on the Platform due to an accessibility barrier, contact us and we will provide reasonable assistance to help you accomplish it through an alternative means where feasible.

6. CONTINUOUS IMPROVEMENT

We welcome feedback and use it to improve. This Statement will be updated as our accessibility efforts progress.
