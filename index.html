Questions families ask

Is this legal advice?

No. HomegoingHQ provides organizational tools and plain-language general information, with clear guidance on when a licensed attorney is genuinely needed — and featured local attorneys who have reviewed our state content.

Who can see our family's information?

Only the people you invite, with the roles you give them. Sensitive numbers can be locked with encryption so strong that even we can't read them.

We're in the middle of it already. Is it too late to start?

Not at all. Most families find us after the funeral. Answer the questions honestly and the roadmap starts exactly where you are.

What if we don't know the answers?

"I'm not sure" is always an acceptable answer — it simply adds a step that helps you find out.
